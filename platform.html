The Campaign Platform

“The test of our progress is whether we provide enough for those who have too little.”

This platform is guided by a simple principle: America must come first in the actions of its government. Not in rhetoric, but in results. The purpose of democratic government is to secure the economic independence, safety, and dignity of its people.

America First Means Economic Security

A nation cannot be strong when its people live in fear of unemployment, debt, or instability. Economic security is not charity — it is the foundation of national independence. Government must act to protect American labor, American industry, and American families from forces they cannot control alone.

This campaign supports policies that place domestic employment, wages, and industrial capacity ahead of speculative finance or foreign dependency.

  • Public investment to sustain employment during economic downturns
  • Protection of American manufacturing and infrastructure
  • Fair labor standards that reward work and productivity

National Recovery Through American Industry

America First requires an economy capable of sustaining itself. Strategic industries essential to national strength — transportation, energy, construction, and manufacturing — must be supported and modernized.

Recovery is not achieved by waiting for markets to correct themselves, but by deliberate national action that restores confidence and capacity.

  • Infrastructure investment using American labor and materials
  • Support for small and mid-sized domestic businesses
  • Long-term planning to prevent future economic collapse

Responsible Government, Not Weak Government

America First does not mean government withdrawal — it means government responsibility. In moments of crisis, inaction is not restraint; it is failure. Democratic government must be capable of acting decisively while remaining accountable to the people.

Fiscal responsibility means planning for the future, not abandoning the present. Public resources must be used carefully, transparently, and with long-term national benefit in mind.

  • Transparent budgeting and public accountability
  • Measured use of public spending to stabilize the economy
  • Protection of retirement and social insurance systems

Integrity, Law, and Democratic Trust

The strength of the Republic depends on trust in its institutions. Public office is a public trust, and no individual or interest stands above the law. Democratic authority must always remain accountable to the people.

America First governance requires honest administration, independent courts, and respect for constitutional limits.

  • Strong ethical standards for public officials
  • Independent oversight and transparent decision-making
  • Respect for constitutional governance and due process

Public Safety and National Stability

Safety at home is essential to opportunity and freedom. Effective public safety institutions must protect communities while preserving civil liberties and the rule of law.

Stability is achieved through professionalism, fairness, and public confidence — not disorder or neglect.

  • Support for professional, accountable law enforcement
  • Fair and consistent application of the law
  • Investment in stable and secure communities

America First in the World

America’s role in the world must serve the security and prosperity of our people. We are not the world police. We should not be funding foreign wars or countries when it does not directly affect the american people. This includes Israel. Through this, we will acquire peace through strenth, and military strength is essential.

Foreign policy should avoid unnecessary conflict while ensuring that American independence and security are never compromised.

  • A strong, prepared national defense
  • Diplomacy grounded in national interest and credibility
  • Avoidance of reckless foreign entanglements
